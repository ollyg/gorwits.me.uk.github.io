About

If you want to get in touch, my public email address is oliver at cpan dot org. I will respond promptly unless on vacation.

Spare time is spent with my family and friends, having a beer, composing EDM, playing board games, cycling, skiing, or walking in the drizzle because it reminds me of Yorkshire. Oh, I also hack a bit in Python and Perl.

I'm a core developer of the awesome Netdisco network management system.
